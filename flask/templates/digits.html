Digit Recognizer

This project uses a Convolutional Neural Network programmed in Tensorflow to recognize a digit drawn in an HTML5 canvas. Give it a try.

Write a digit 0-9, in the box below and click submit.

Unfortunately, your browser isn't currently unsupported.

The model was trained on batches of 100, for 20,000 iterations, resulting in a 97% accuracy.

Preprocessing is needed to center and transform the image into a 784 dimensional vector.
